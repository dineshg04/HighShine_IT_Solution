Marketing Innovation – YouTube Channel

Instead of relying only on crowded channels like LinkedIn and Google Ads, Highshine can use YouTube to share short educational videos about common ERP challenges such as failed implementations, budget overruns, poor user training, and lack of post-launch support. These videos would provide practical advice from real project experience rather than direct sales pitches, helping build trust with potential clients.

This approach fits Highshine's positioning as a long-term implementation and support partner. Success can be measured through video views, watch time, website visits from YouTube links, and lead generation. The goal is not to reach millions of viewers, but to attract the right audience—IT managers, CEO, Founders, and business decision-makers researching ERP solutions.
