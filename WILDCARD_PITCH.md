PRODUCT IMPROVEMENT PITCH

Observation:

After reviewing the website, the main issue is not the lack of information but how it is presented. The design feels text-heavy in some sections, the visual hierarchy could be stronger, and visitors may not immediately understand what Highshine offers or where to go next. For a B2B company, first impressions are critical because decision-makers often spend only a few seconds evaluating a vendor. I would make the website more attractive and give a better user experience.

What I Would Build:

I would implement an AI-powered website chatbot that acts as a virtual consultant. Instead of forcing visitors to search through multiple pages, the chatbot would instantly answer questions about ERP services, implementation support, pricing inquiries, industries served, and contact information. It would also guide users to relevant pages and collect qualified leads.

Timeline

Design chatbot flow and knowledge base: 2-3 days
Frontend integration and UI implementation: 2 days
Backend integration and testing: 2 days

Total time: 5–7 days

 Success Metrics

1. Increase in average session duration
2. Reduction in website bounce rate
3. Number of chatbot interactions per week
4. Number of qualified leads generated through the chatbot
5. Increase in contact and consultation requests

The goal is to make the website more interactive, easier to navigate, and more effective at converting visitors into potential clients.
