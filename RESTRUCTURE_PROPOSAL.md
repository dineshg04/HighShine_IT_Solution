RESTRUCTURE PROPOSAL

Objective is to improve user engagement and increase clients.

1. Move Client Success Stories Higher

Place customer success stories and project outcomes closer to the top of the page.

Why: Visitors trust real results and client experiences more than service descriptions.

2. Highlight Post-Implementation Support

Make Highshine's long-term support offering more visible in important content areas.

Why: Ongoing support is a major difference that sets Highshine apart from many ERP providers.

3. Simplify Technical Content

Reduce lengthy product descriptions and provide concise summaries with "Learn More" options.

Why: Shorter content improves readability and helps visitors focus on key information.

4. Add an AI Chatbot

Implement a chatbot to answer common questions and guide users to relevant services.

Why: It improves navigation, enhances user experience, and helps generate more qualified leads.

Expected Outcome

A clearer website structure, better user engagement, and increased consultation requests and lead conversions.
