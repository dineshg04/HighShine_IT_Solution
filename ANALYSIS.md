
Based on the visitor data, most users spend time on the homepage but fewer continue to deeper service pages. This suggests that visitors are interested in the company but may not immediately understand the value ant confusion in what next action to take.

Recommendations 

1: Move Client Success Stories Higher

Users are more likely to engage when they see proof of successful projects. Moving testimonials and case studies closer to the top can build trust earlier and encourage visitors to explore further.


2: Strengthen Calls-to-Action

Clearer and more visible "Book a Consultation" or "Contact Us" buttons should be placed throughout the page. This helps convert interested visitors before they leave the website.

3: Add an AI Chatbot

Some visitors leave without exploring multiple pages. An AI chatbot can answer questions instantly, guide users to relevant services, and improve lead generation by capturing visitor inquiries in real time.

Expected Impact

These changes should improve user engagement, increase time spent on the website, reduce bounce rates, and generate more qualified leads.
